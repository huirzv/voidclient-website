About Void Client

Void Client is a modern Minecraft client designed to enhance your gameplay experience with powerful customization options, performance improvements, a mod marketplace and a way to upload mods directly to the client, and unique features not found in the vanilla game.

Our mission is to provide players with tools to make Minecraft truly their own, while maintaining the essence of what makes the game great. Whether you're a competitive player looking for performance gains or a creative player wanting more customization, Void Client has something for everyone.

Key Features:

  • Completely customizable HUD and GUI elements
  • Built-in FPS optimization and performance monitoring
  • Mod marketplace with one click uploading to the client
  • Advanced screenshot and uploading tools
  • Friend system to connect with other players
  • Sleek, modern user interface
  • Regular updates with new features and improvements
  • Lightweight design that won't slow down your game

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Void Client free to use?
A: Yes, we offer paid subscriptions as well as free to use perks as well. On top of this, we're open source.

Q: What versions of Minecraft does Void Client support?
A: We currently support Minecraft versions 1.8.9 through 1.20.1.

Q: When I launch the Volt Client Setup, why does it show Windows Protected My PC?
A: Sadly, Microsoft charges $200 for license keys and has to go through a larger process to become a verified publisher, so for now, as a temporary fix we use this until we can become published. Void Client can assure you though that it is safe. Simply click more details and run anyway.

Q: How do I install mods from the marketplace?
A: Currently, our custom marketplace is under development, so for now we use modrinth to house our mods. Find mods there, and simply upload them to the launcher!
